About Us

Our mission is to improve your obstacle racing training by proving as much quality information to you as possible. We will search for the latest information and read the articles for you and just give you all the information you need to know for your next training in an easy and quick read format. Our goal here at Obstacle Racing Insights© is to help you improve your obstacle and overall fitness knowledge, leading to better training and race results.

We are obstacle runners ourselves and like to share this passion with you by creating as much valuable content about the sport by uploading blogs regularly. The blogs cover all aspects of the sport, including training methods, specific exercises and obstacle guides, nutrition tips, physiology of muscles, recipes, obstacle race gear reviews, race reviews and many more! Together we will become better obstacle racers!

ORI Staff

Luuk
I always have been active, doing multiple sports. I started with tennis and soccer and later switched to the gym and rowing. Since I was 15, I started going to the gym to get stronger and gain some muscles. But after some years it started to become somewhat dull. The goal of going to the gym is often to look good, but the muscles or looks do not provide you with useful physical capabilities. I did not feel I was getting fitter by doing only strength exercises, but I don’t enjoy doing cardio in the gym. Thus, I went outside for cardio and started running next to the strength training. Then, by accident we stumbled upon a Strong Viking race and we were immediately enthusiastic about it. I started with obstacle running in 2017 and after my first obstacle run, I immediately fell in love with it. As I am a competitive person by nature, I started focussed on the competitive aspect of the sport.
