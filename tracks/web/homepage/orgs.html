ABOUT STRONG VIKING

Strong Viking is an organization created by Founder & CEO Jan Reijs and a group of entrepreneurs with backgrounds in both extreme sports and the military. Together they share a great enthusiasm and love for the sport of obstacle running. As a company, we have the goal to organize the biggest & best obstacle runs in the World. Quality, safety and innovation are the keys factors of our ambitious project.

Strong Viking is based on the history of the Vikings. Vikings were known to be Strong, Tough and adventurous people that shared a great sense of Brotherhood. These are exactly the elements that are central to the vision our vision. The goal of Strong Viking is to push the participants to the limits of their mental and physical abilities so that they will become stronger not only at Strong Viking but in their daily lives.

Strong Viking provides their participants with a unique experience. Our obstacle run is not a race but a challenge. Our participants suffer together, but with a smile on their faces, and that’s what Strong Viking is all about!

The Vikings conquered many lands and expanded their origins throughout Europe and the World. So who knows, you may have a bit of Viking DNA in you! Join our tribe and find out!

ABOUT SPARTAN

It all started in the Green Mountains of Vermont when Spartan founder, Joe De Sena, became increasingly aware that humans are missing a critical component in the modern world. As we took steps forward technologically, holistic health took a step backward. Life is getting “easier”, but as a result, people are becoming overweight, depressed, lonely and less self-sufficient.

Inspired by the warrior society of ancient Sparta, he set out to create a community of Spartans who push and inspire each other. Our Spartan obstacle courses seek to manufacture adversity, which then trains your body and mind to overcome obstacles on the course and in your everyday lives. Spartans develop a resilient spirit, a capable body and mind, all supported and driven by an incredible community.

After our first obstacle course race in 2010, Spartan has grown to 250+ annual events across 42+ countries.

For more information on Spartan, visit their website: Spartan

ABOUT MUD MASTERS

A CHALLENGING OBSTACLE COURSE, BASED ON MILITARY OBSTACLE COURSES!

You’ll escape the daily grind on our physically and mentally challenging course and – together with your friends – you’ll conquer the most spectacular obstacles. For one day, you will be the action hero you always wanted to be.

The difference distances of 6, 12, 16, 21 and 42 kilometers, make it a realistic challenge for many people. At Mud Masters Obstacle Run, a fast finish time is not important; it’s all about making it to the finish line together. And when that happens, it is time to celebrate that accomplishment with a well-deserved finish beer. Work hard, play hard!
